About Mphela Academy

Mphela Academy is a skills development institution focused on practical, hands-on training in art, jewellery making, beadwork, and entrepreneurship. We empower youth, women, unemployed individuals, and people with disabilities through creative education that builds real income opportunities.

Why This Matters

South Africa faces high unemployment and limited access to practical skills training. Mphela Academy bridges this gap by teaching real-world craft skills combined with business knowledge, enabling learners to create income and start small businesses.
